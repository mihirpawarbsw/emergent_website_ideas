Core Capabilities

A unified workspace of eleven intelligence modules.

Every module works standalone and together — Retrieval-Augmented Generation, Knowledge Graphs, Semantic Search, NLP, Web Intelligence, Visual Analytics, Timeline Analysis, Action Extraction, and Executive Reporting inside one coherent platform.

01 · RAG Conversational Intelligence

Ask across your entire knowledge base.

Complex questions, cross-document comparisons, summaries, evidence retrieval, and contextual reasoning — every response grounded in retrieved document chunks, not hallucinated.

02 · Knowledge Graph

See what connects — across everything.

Automatically identify entities and relationships. Visualize how people, organizations, concepts, products, and locations connect across an entire document collection. Reveal what manual reading never could.

03 · Semantic Search

Search meaning, not keywords.

Locate information based on intent, context, synonyms, and relationships — across multiple uploaded documents simultaneously.

04 · Multi-Document Intelligence

Compare, merge, contradict.

Analyze multiple documents together. Compare findings, identify overlaps, detect contradictions, and merge scattered knowledge into unified insights.

05 · Executive Report Generator

Leadership-ready reports, on demand.

Automatically generate comprehensive executive reports summarizing key findings, business insights, critical observations, strategic recommendations, risks, opportunities, and supporting evidence — structured for decision makers.

06 · Cluster Universe

Organize by meaning.

Documents naturally group by semantic similarity. Related documents form knowledge clusters while unrelated content stays apart — providing a high-level view of massive collections.

07 · TimelineWeave

How information evolves over time.

Extract chronological events, combine document timelines with external context, and track progression across projects, research, policies, and reports.

08 · TrendLens · Web Intelligence

Validate internal knowledge with the outside world.

Compare uploaded documents against live external intelligence — news, research, industry trends, and market developments. Identify validation, contradictions, emerging trends, and missing context.

09 · Visual Pulse

Concepts you can see.

Turn important themes, ideas, products, and entities into visual representations for rapid comprehension across large collections.

10 · StatSonar

Numbers, distributions, dashboards.

Statistical summaries and analytical dashboards from document-derived information — metrics, frequencies, and trend surfaces at a glance.

11 · LexiScope · NLP

Deep linguistic understanding.

Keyword extraction, topic modeling, entity recognition, sentiment, phrase discovery, concept extraction, language analytics, and document statistics.

Action · Tracker

Every commitment, captured.

Automatically identify action items, tasks, owners, deadlines, commitments, decisions, and follow-ups — then organize them into structured workflows ready for execution.
